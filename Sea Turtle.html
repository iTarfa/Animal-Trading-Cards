Sea Turtle

The leatherback sea turtle is the only sea turtle that does not have a hard shell. Instead, it bears a mosaic of bony plates beneath its leathery skin.

  • Kingdom: Animalia.
  • Phylum: Chordata.
  • Class: Reptilia.
  • Order: Testudines.

The majority of a sea turtle's body is protected by its shell. The sea turtle's shell is divided into two sections: the carapace and the plastron. The shell is made up of smaller plates called scutes.
